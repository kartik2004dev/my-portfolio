01. ABOUT ME

The Intersection of Design & Defense

I’m Kartik Patel, a UI/UX Designer with a Diploma in Computer Science. My philosophy is simple: technology should be accessible, beautiful, and inherently safe.

While many designers focus solely on aesthetics, I bring a unique technical edge. I have completed my CEH (Certified Ethical Hacker) certification, which empowers me to design products with a deeper understanding of security protocols and user trust dynamics.

Currently, I’m expanding my expertise into Digital Forensics. This study of data, investigations, and system behavior informs my design decisions.
